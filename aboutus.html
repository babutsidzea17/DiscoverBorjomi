About Borjomi

Borjomi is a jewel of Georgia, nestled in the lush forests of the Lesser Caucasus mountains. Famous for its mineral waters, healing climate, and stunning natural beauty, Borjomi has been a beloved destination for centuries — once favored by Russian royalty and Georgian nobility alike. The town is best known for its naturally carbonated spring water, which flows from deep within volcanic rock and is bottled and sold worldwide. But there`s far more to Borjomi than its iconic water. Visitors are drawn to the peaceful atmosphere, scenic trails, ancient landmarks, and therapeutic resorts that make Borjomi a true sanctuary for rest and rejuvenation. Borjomi is also the gateway to the Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park — one of the largest protected areas in Europe — where travelers can explore diverse ecosystems, encounter rare wildlife, and enjoy some of Georgia`s most breathtaking landscapes. Rich in culture and hospitality, Borjomi welcomes all who seek a blend of nature, wellness, and tradition. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or an extended retreat, Borjomi offers experiences that nourish both body and soul. Come discover why Borjomi is not just a place — it's a feeling you'll never forget.
