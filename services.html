Getting to Borjomi:

Borjomi is easily accessible from Tbilisi and other major cities in Georgia.

  • By car: A scenic 2-hour drive from Tbilisi via the E60 highway.
  • By train: Daily trains run from Tbilisi to Borjomi, offering a comfortable and affordable option.
  • By bus or minibus (marshrutka): Regular departures from Didube Station in Tbilisi. From Kutaisi: Buses and shared taxis are available from Kutaisi International Airport.
