Borjomi Park Rehabilitation Has Begun!

17.06.2025

Borjomi Central Park is undergoing rehabilitation! The project includes restoring old paths, improving infrastructure, and adding new green spaces. Construction has already started and is expected to finish by year-end. The renewed park will offer locals and tourists a more beautiful and comfortable place to relax and explore.

Concert Coming to Borjomi!

18.07.2025

A major open-air concert will take place in Borjomi`s Central Park this weekend. Local and international artists will perform live starting at 6 PM. The event is free and open to all. Join us for a night of music, fun, and celebration in the heart of Borjomi!
