Nestled in the heart of Georgia, Borjomi is a breathtaking blend of lush forests, healing mineral waters, and rich cultural heritage. Known worldwide for its natural springs, this charming town offers relaxation, adventure, and history in one unforgettable destination. Explore the famous Borjomi Central Park, hike through the Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park, or simply enjoy the crisp mountain air and stunning views. Whether you're here for wellness, nature, or discovery, Borjomi welcomes you with open arms and endless beauty. Discover why generations have fallen in love with Georgia`s most iconic spa town.
